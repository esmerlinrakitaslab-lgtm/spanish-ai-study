Learning to trust the machine: The psychological relationship forming in human-AI language learning

Rowan University · SURP 2026

What we are studying

People are starting to learn languages by talking with conversational AI. This study asks a psychological question about that: do learners form a real bond with an AI that teaches them, and does that bond change how brave they are about speaking?

Fifteen intermediate Spanish learners will practice out loud with an AI voice tutor for three months. Each week they record a short first-person video of a practice session, and each month they sit down with a researcher to watch parts of it back and talk about what they felt.

The study at a glance

WhoAdults 18+, intermediate (about B1) Spanish
